How to disable your adblocker for theguardian.com

Thank you for expressing an interest in disabling your ad blocker on theguardian.com.

The Guardian rely on advertising revenues to keep funding fearless, high-quality and honest journalism. In this way, ad revenues help ensure our continued independence. The method for disabling your adblocker is relatively consistent across the different combinations; press the icon in your toolbar/status bar for the ad blocker that you use (or otherwise access its settings), and then select the option to ‘disable on theguardian.com’ (wording may vary). Please see below for instructions for the most popular browser/extension combinations on desktop devices. Once your ad blocker is disabled, the next page that you load will contain ads and you will be helping to sustain the Guardian in perpetuity.

User experience is always of paramount importance to us at the Guardian. Our ad spaces are non-intrusive and browsing disruption is kept at a minimum. You won't see pop-up or full page adverts here.

If you do see an ad that you feel is intrusive or makes accessing the Guardian problematic, please send details to userhelp@@theguardian.com, including if possible a screen grab, the country you are accessing the Guardian from and the browser and device you are using.

Thank you!
