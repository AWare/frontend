Chelsea Manning release

What happens to Chelsea Manning now she has been released?

What kind of life does she emerge into? Manning enters the civilian world as a celebrity transgender woman who is regarded as a hero on the left and within the LGBT community, but a traitor among conservatives and many in the military.

Where will she live? She has indicated she wants to return to Maryland, where she was based before she entered the army.

What is her military status? Her conviction remains in place, and as a result her active military status. Her lawyers are continuing to appeal her conviction and until that process is completed she will not be discharged from the army.

How will she make ends meet? There are several fundraising efforts underway to help her transition to civilian life and cover her legal fees. She will be in demand as a commentator and speaker.
